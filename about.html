Our Services

Pottery Night Experience

designed for adults

Wheel Classes

six week duration

$75/class/person plus GST *includes one finished piece, clay, tools

We host these most Saturdays and Sundays at 4 & 6 pm. Each class is 90 minutes.

You will be given a demonstration and then go step by step along with the instructor, we try our very best to have a successful piece. There’s bit of clean up and then you choose the glaze colour to finish your piece with.

You have the fun, we’ll do the work & call you when it’s done! It takes somewhere around 3 weeks to finish all the firing and glazing.

One finished piece is included in your event fee. You are able to purchase your additional piece(s) for $15/piece.

After receiving your pick up call request, we will hold your pieces for 30 days.

$280 for the 6 weeks, $44.95 for clay, and $26.95 for our tool kit.

Cancelations & Refunds

1. Rebook: If you have already paid for a future course we will assist you to rebook into the future.

2. Convert to Gift Voucher: We can also convert your purchase into a gift voucher which would be valid for two years. This would allow you to apply it to a future course, clay, tools, a pottery night experience or purchasing pieces from our shop.

3. Refund: If you wish to request a refund for (a 6-week course, clay & tools), (a 6-week course & clay) or (a 6-week course) a $50 admin fee will be held back.

4. if you are cancelling a Pottery Night Event, please do so 7 days in advance for full refund. Within 7 days we will try to rebook you

5. Late Policy - Pottery Night Events start on the hour, if you are 15+ minutes late, we will rebook you for another evening

If you have paid for a course in advance and wish to exercise one of these options please email us at kj@vivaclayworks.com. This will assist us in keeping your requests organized.
